WHY IT MATTERS

What your schema is
quietly costing you

Most database problems are invisible until they're expensive. These are the risks already sitting in your schema — you just haven't found them yet.

🔓

A breach waiting to happen

Sensitive data stored in ways that bypass encryption entirely. It won't show up in a code review. It will show up in a headline.

📉

Data your reports can't trust

Corrupted relationships and missing constraints let invalid data accumulate silently. By the time you notice, the damage is already in production.

🐢

Queries that collapse under load

The missing indexes aren't a problem at 1,000 users. At 100,000 they become your biggest outage — and the hardest one to explain.

📋

The compliance audit you're not ready for

Healthcare and finance schemas carry structural requirements most developers don't know exist — until a regulator asks for documentation you don't have.

🧱

Design that blocks your next hire

Schemas built without standards create onboarding debt. New engineers spend weeks reverse-engineering structure instead of shipping features.

🕳️

Silent data loss with no error thrown

Referential gaps that let records vanish or orphan without raising an exception. Your application keeps running. Your data doesn't stay whole.
